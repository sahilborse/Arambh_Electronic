About Us




Team Vision



“To be pioneers in electric F1 prototype car engineering, pushing the boundaries of performance and sustainability. Our vision is to create cutting-edge, electric racing machines that lead the way in the world of motorsport, demonstrating the future of high-speed, eco-friendly racing technology through innovation, expertise, and passion.”

Team goals ​



Prototype Excellence: Build a series of high-performance electric F1 prototypes that demonstrate technical prowess and innovation.​ ​
Sustainability Leadership: Strive to be a leader in sustainable racing by optimizing energy efficiency and promoting eco-friendly practices.​ ​​
Technical Innovation: Continuously innovate and develop cutting-edge technologies that can be applied in both racing and everyday electric vehicles.​
